Inventory Policy
Restocking Recommendations

Provided that Inventory is both a valuable resource and a source of waste, it is imperative to have an Inventory Policy that set the guidelines of how much inventory to keep on hand, when to order new stock and, how to handle backorders.

Efficient Inventory Policies can minimize the cost of: Stock outs, Carrying costs, Material purchases and, Storage costs.
