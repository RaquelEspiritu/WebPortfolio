Inventory Policy
Restocking Recommendations

Imagine it is Monday morning and you need to report the current Inventory status to different levels of the organization. The Inventory Health Dashboard showed below can help you to achieve exactly that. The report starts by showing you 4 KPI's: Total Inventory, Sales, Forecarts and Forecast Accuracy, the values are expressed in unis and can be filtered by SKU number. Notice the KPI's compare the values of the current week versus the previous one, in addition, in the line chart showed at the bottom of each KPI you can check the historical values of previous weeks.

As you scroll down in the report, answers to important questions can be found, questions such as:

How much inventory we currently have in the US, by State and in each store? What has been the Sales behaviour during the past weeks and what can we expect the behaviour to be in the future? What items do we need to put more attention to? How mmuch inventory do we need to procure in order to provide the desired customer service levels (ROPSS)? When do we need those units and where do they need to be placed to?

Click on the Dashboard's image to download the Tableau report.

Note: All values, places and products showed in the report are fictitious, and are used for illustrative purposes only.
