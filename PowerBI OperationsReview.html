Business Performance Overview

How would you like to stay on top of your business all the time? The Dashboard showed below helps you visualize the KPI's of various departments so that they can be kept under control.

The report starts with the General Performance for 2021, here you can find the On Time Delivery %'s by month according to the customer's Requested Data and Promised Date. Total Customer Orders for 2021 in USD, how much of those orders where shipped and Invoiced and what was the Total Order Backlog in USD as well. Moreover, you can find what was the Company's Marked Share at the end of that year. What were the Productivity levels for 2021 per month as well as of the First Pass Yield.

As you move to other pages or the report you will be able to find specific KPI's for Sales/Customer Service, Quality and Manufacturing.

Click on the Dashboard's image to download the Power BI report.
