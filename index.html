About Me

Hello there! My name is Raquel Espiritu and I am glad to see you in here. Let me tell you a little bit about myself:

My educational background includes an APICS Certification as a Supply Chain Professional, a Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Engineering, Masters of Business Administration in Finance and, in a few more weeks, a Master of Science in Business Analytics. My professional career has been focused in Supply Chain Management.

During these years I have covered an array of job positions that went from Quality Engineer, Buyer, Planner, Customer Service Representative, Master Scheduler, Supply Chain Analyst to Global Inventory Manager. This allowed me to develop an end-to-end vision of the Supply Chain, its inputs, outputs, processes, KPI's and the impact of each one in the bottom line.

Some of my specialties are (but are not limited to):

  • Project Management.
  • Supply Chain Engineering.
  • Strong working knowledge of all supply chain information and transactional systems.
  • Solid business acumen, coupled with the ability to communicate to all levels of the orgeanization.
  • Demonstrated ability to multi-task, work under pressure, think analytically and communicate effectively.
  • Detail oriented and accurate.
  • Adaptability and team work.

In the following pages you will find some business cases that illustrate the kind of projects I have developed. Feel free to contact me should you have any questions.
